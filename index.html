Ratna Chuli

    Have you ever wanted to travel to Nepal and climb a mountain? Do you enjoy physical and mental challenges? If so, Ratna Chuli is the mountain for you!


    Ratna Chuli is located on the Peri Himal NW of the Manaslu Himal in Nepal. Situated right between Annapurna and Manaslu, Ratna Chuli is a majestic sight to behold when seen from the Tibetan village of Phu. The mountain is roughly 7000 meters tall and has proved itself to be a technical climb for those who chose to ascend it. Ever since the mountain was opened for climbing, very few mountaineering expedition teams have taken on the challenge to summit its slopes.

    Quick Facts:
    Elevation: 7128 m
    Country: China/Nepal
    Difficulty: Major Mountain Expedition
    First Year Climbed: 1996
    First Successful Climbers: Santa Bahadur Ale, Durga Bahadur Tamang, Tul Bahadur Tamang, Katsuhiko Sawada, Osamu Tanabe( climbing leader)
    Recommended Expedition Size: 4-5 people
